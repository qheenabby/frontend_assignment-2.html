A. BROWNIES

This is my favourite recipe

List Of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Nutella
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Put flour inside a bowl
  2. Crack three eggs
  3. Pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degrees celsius for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy!

B. EGUSI SOUP WITH SEMO

List Of Ingredients

  • Egusi
  • Palmoil
  • Assorted Meat
  • Dry fish/Stock fish/crayfish
  • Yellow pepper/Onions
  • Uziza leaf

Steps:

  1. Season your assorted meat with maggi,salt and onions to taste
  2. Heat your palm oil and add your mashed egusi with onions
  3. Stir for few minutes then add your meat stock with your dryfish,stockfish
  4. Add your grinded pepper,crayfish
  5. Add your maggi and salt to taste
  6. Stir and allow it cook for few minutes
  7. Add your leaf and allow to steam
  8. Finally! Soup is ready
  9. With your hot water prepare your semolina
  10. Serve and Enjoy!

C. YAM & EGG SAUCE

List Of Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Fresh tomatoes and pepper,onions
  • Carrot,Green pepper
  • Yam

Steps:

  1. Cut all your vegetables in desired size
  2. Mix your desired number of eggs
  3. Fry your oil,add your onion,tomato,red pepper
  4. Stir and allow to steam
  5. Add your carrot and green pepper and stir
  6. Add maggi,salt to taste,curry and thyme(optional)
  7. Add your whisked egg and stir
  8. Egg sauce is ready
  9. Slice your yam in desired shape and boil with salt to taste
  10. Yam and Egg sauce ready to serve and enjoy.

D. AFANG SOUP

List Of Ingredients.

  • Afang-leaf
  • Water-leaf
  • Assorted meat
  • Periwinkle
  • Stockfish/Crayfish/Dryfish
  • Palm-oil/maggi/salt

Steps:

  1. Season your washed assorted meat,stockfish,dryfish
  2. Wash your waterleaf to avoid sand
  3. Steam your palmoil and add your waterleaf with sliced onions immediately
  4. Add your assorted meat stock,grinded pepper and crayfish
  5. Add your maggi and salt to taste,add a good amount of water
  6. Allow to cook for few minutes
  7. Soup is ready!

E. YAM PORRIDGE

List Of Ingredients.

  • Yam
  • Palm-oil
  • Dry-fish
  • Fresh Pepper
  • Maggi/salt

Steps:

  1. Cook your yam to be half-done
  2. When almost done add your dryfish,crayfish(optional)
  3. Add your pepper,maggi and salt to taste
  4. If too dry you can add little water,then allow it cook for few more minutes
  5. Part of the yam can be mashed if you want it so
  6. Food is ready!
